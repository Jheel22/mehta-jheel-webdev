Project

  • E-Commerce Website

  • MAY-JUNE 2017

The website is built using MEAN Stack. I have used Bootstrap 3.0 for the UI and AngularJs in combination with REST services and JQuery for the backend.

Small time entrepreneurs do not have a platform to advertise/sell their products or services. This website will allow users to display their products or list their services for other registered user to buy, review or view. The user can create their own pages to sell their products or services. Users can follow other users, connect with them via other social media and also chat. Different users will create pages(like a website) to list their products/services for sale. Major user goals are to display their products/services, Find all types of products/services to buy or view, Connect with people in the same business for expansion
